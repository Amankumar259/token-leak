# SpendPilot Business Analysis

## Problem

AI tooling costs are growing rapidly across startups and engineering teams.

Organizations often:

- overspend on premium plans
- duplicate subscriptions
- misuse enterprise tooling
- lack visibility into AI infrastructure spend

---

## Solution

SpendPilot audits AI tooling usage and generates:

- optimization recommendations
- savings estimates
- AI-generated summaries
- actionable spend reduction opportunities

- product organizations
